Where does Miami Ohio basketball play its home games?

Miami Ohio basketball plays its home games at Millett Hall, a multi-purpose arena located on the Miami University campus in Oxford, Ohio. The venue opened in 1968 and has a seating capacity of 9,200 spectators, according to Miami RedHawks Facilities Information.

Millett Hall serves as the primary home court for both the men's and women's basketball teams, providing a consistent venue for practices and games. The arena is also utilized for other university events, including concerts and commencement ceremonies.
